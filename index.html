WELCOME TO FOLAM IDEAL SCHOOL

The school runs the Nigerian curriculum. The academic curriculum of the college is divided into two segments; namely; the Junior Secondary school and the Senior Secondary School in strict compliance with the National Policy on Education.

In the first three years, all students follow a common curriculum which provides widely-based courses centered on the acquisition of study skills and fundamentals which prepares them for the demand of the third form examination.

The subjects offered are English language, Mathematics, Business Studies, Basic Science, Basic technology, Home Economics, Art, French Computer Studies, Religious Studies, Music, and Agricultural Science.

EXAMINATION OFFERED

The College is a government approved international private school. It is accredited by the Ogun State Government and the Federal Government of the Republic of Nigeria to instruct and train students for the following examinations:

1. Junior Secondary School examination (BECE, checkpoint) 2. Senior Secondary School Certificate Examination (WAEC, NECO)
